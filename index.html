PhaseBloom Privacy Policy & Support

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This page contains both the Privacy Policy and Support information for the PhaseBloom app.

Privacy Policy

PhaseBloom respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide directly, such as notes, cycle-related entries, and reminder preferences.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate the app, such as app version and device type.
  • Crash and diagnostic data to improve app stability (if enabled).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features and reminders.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with service providers when necessary to operate the app (for example, crash reporting), and only under appropriate safeguards.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service, resolve issues, or comply with legal obligations.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
