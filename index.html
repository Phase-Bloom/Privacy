Official App Privacy Page

Privacy Policy, AI Notice & Support

Last updated: April 2026

This page applies to the PhaseBloom mobile application for users in the European Union, United States, and other supported regions.

PhaseBloom works locally on your device, does not require an account, and does not use a backend to store your personal entries.
No account required.
Your entries stay on your device.
No cloud backend for personal data.
Anonymous diagnostics via Sentry only.

1. Privacy Policy

PhaseBloom is designed with privacy in mind. Personal information you enter, such as cycle entries, mood logs, notes, reminders, and preferences, is stored locally on your device.

This information is not uploaded to our servers and is not accessible by the developer.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Cycle data
  • Mood entries
  • Notes
  • Energy levels
  • Reminder settings
  • App preferences

3. Crash Reports & Diagnostics

To improve app stability and troubleshoot issues, PhaseBloom uses Sentry, a third-party crash reporting service.

Limited technical data may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information

Personal notes, mood entries, and cycle data are not intentionally transmitted through crash reporting.

4. How Information Is Used

  • To provide the app’s core functionality
  • To store your entries locally on your device
  • To improve reliability, stability, and performance

5. Data Sharing

PhaseBloom does not sell or share your personal data.

Limited technical diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry solely for crash reporting and troubleshooting.

6. Data Retention

Your personal data remains on your device until you delete it manually or uninstall the app.

Because PhaseBloom does not require an account, there is no server-side profile or cloud account storing your entries.

7. Your Choices

You may delete your data at any time by removing entries in the app or uninstalling PhaseBloom.

8. AI Notice

PhaseBloom does not currently use AI to make medical decisions, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations.

If AI-supported features are added in the future, this page will be updated to explain what the feature does, what data it uses, and important limitations.

9. Medical Disclaimer

PhaseBloom is intended for wellness, self-reflection, and informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or contraception guidance.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
