PhaseBloom Privacy Policy & Support

Last updated: April 2026

This page contains both the Privacy Policy and Support information for the PhaseBloom app.

Privacy Policy

PhaseBloom is designed with privacy in mind. The app works locally on your device and does not require an account.

All personal data you enter (such as notes, mood, and cycle data) stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

1. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Cycle data, notes, mood, energy levels, and reminder settings are stored locally on your device.
  • This data is not uploaded, shared, or accessible by the developer.

2. Diagnostic & Crash Data

PhaseBloom uses a third-party service (Sentry) to collect anonymous crash and diagnostic data. This helps identify bugs and improve app stability.

  • No personal notes or cycle data are transmitted.
  • Technical information such as device type, OS version, and error logs may be sent.

3. Data Usage

  • To provide core app functionality.
  • To improve performance and reliability.

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared. Diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry solely for crash reporting purposes.

5. Data Retention

All personal data remains on your device until you delete the app or remove the data manually.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
